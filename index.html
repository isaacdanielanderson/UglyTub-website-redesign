Refinishing Your Tub

Bathtub Refinishing is very Cost Effective bathroom restoration alternative to replacing a bathtub that is stained, damaged and very hard to clean. Bath Magics will restore the surface of the bathtub to make it look and feel as if you just bought it brand new. You will save thousands of dollars and avoid days and even weeks from demolishing it with a costly replacement.

Bathtubs, Cast Iron, Fiberglass

Bathtubs come in all materials, and age in different ways. We are experts in repairing worn surfaces on bathtubs and showers. Even if it has been previously refinished, we can work with it. We do residential and commercial work. Bathtub refinishing is a cost effective bathroom makeover alternative to replacing a bathtub that is worn out, damaged, hard to clean, or simply the wrong color.

Showers and Bath Showers

Give your shower a bright, new appearance by refinishing the stained and worn fiberglass shower pan. Extend the life of your bathtubs, tile and countertops for many years! Our finish is strong, durable, and easy to care for. No more scrubbing with harsh cleaners. Most jobs take just a few hours to complete.
